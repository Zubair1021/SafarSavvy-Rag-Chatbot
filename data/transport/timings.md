 # Timings & Service Calendar

 ## Weekday Service (Mon–Fri)
 - Operating Hours: 06:45 – 20:30
 - Peak Hours: 07:00–10:00, 16:00–19:00 (increased frequency)
 - Non-Peak: Regular frequency as per route

 ## Weekend Service (Sat)
 - Operating Hours: 08:00 – 18:00
 - Frequency: Every 40–60 minutes (selected routes only)

 ## Sunday / Holidays
 - No regular service unless notified
 - Check updates on the portal

 ## Exam Period Adjustments
 - Temporary schedules published 1 week in advance
 - Late buses added on demand (subject to notice)
